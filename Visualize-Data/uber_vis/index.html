Uber Surge Pricing

Uber Technologies Inc. is an American multinational mobile ride hail company. Uber rates increase to ensure reliability when demand cannot be met by the number of drivers on the road. We can access fair estimate and surge pricing data via the Uber API. Below is a one week chart (Jan 24, 2016 - Jan 31, 2016) showing, in 5 minute intervals, the average surge rates divided into weekday and weekend buckets going from NYC's Penn Station to NYU in Greenwich Village. It clearly shows a increase during the weekday morning commute and the weekend evenings. Despite those trends, surge pricing is surprisingly volatile.

Avg. Uber Surge Rate in 5 Minute Increments Between NYC's Penn Station & NYU (week of Jan 24, 2016)
